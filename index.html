Valproic Acid Correction Calculator

In blood, most drugs are bound to serum proteins. The unbound (free) fraction is pharmacologically active. Valproic acid binding depends on albumin and total drug concentration. Use this calculator to estimate the pharmacologically active (free) VPA in patients, especially in hypoalbuminemia.
